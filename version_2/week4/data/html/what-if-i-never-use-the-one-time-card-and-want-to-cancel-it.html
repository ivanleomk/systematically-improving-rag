What if I never use the One-time card and want to cancel it?

The One-time card will expire if you don\xe2\x80\x99t use it at the store\xe2\x80\x99s checkout within 24 hours after it\xe2\x80\x99s created. You can also cancel the One-time card instantly if it\xe2\x80\x99s no longer needed. When either of these actions occurs, we will cancel any payment plan you might have, including the service fee.

To cancel an unused One-time card:

  1. Go to

    Wallet

  2. Select the card or payment plan you want to cancel

  3. Tap Cancel this card

Good to know: If you want to make another purchase, just create a new card and keep on shopping.
